Privacy Policy - Remember App

Privacy Policy for Remember App

Last updated: September 2025

Our Commitment

This app is committed to protecting your privacy by storing all data locally on your device. We do not use any cloud services or external storage solutions.

What is stored in the app

  • Memories and personal notes
  • App settings and preferences
  • Avatar state and customizations
  • Achievement data

How we store it

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is ever uploaded to any cloud service
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • Backups are created only when you request them

Your data is yours

  • All data stays on your device
  • No internet connection required
  • Backup files are verified for security
  • You have complete control over your data

What we store in the database

To provide essential functionality and support, the following information may be stored in our database:

  • User ID
  • Your referral count
  • Who referred you
  • The items you own (avatars and decorations)

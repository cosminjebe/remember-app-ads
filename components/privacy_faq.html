FAQ

Are my memories private?

Yes. Everything is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or track your content.

What is a Wholesome Memory?

A daily highlight you can mark; your avatar can resurface it in surprise moments.

How does the avatar grow?

By collecting Energy through light engagement and milestones. It evolves across 5 life stages.

Are there ads?

No intrusive ads. Watching ads is optional and only gives bonus rewards.

Can I switch phones?

Yes. Use manual JSON backup/restore from Settings to move your memories and progress.
